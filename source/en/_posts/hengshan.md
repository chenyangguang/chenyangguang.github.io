[](#Hengshan-I'm-Coming)Hengshan I'm Coming
First heard of Hengshan when learning geography in second year of middle school. Had to take exams, memorize books, made up a doggerel: "East West South North Central, Qin Hua Heng Heng Song." Moreover, these five mountains that tourists have talked about since ancient times, the longing in my heart, finally came too late.
This time ran to Hengshan to climb. Didn't want to squeeze into crowds, but wanted to come out to relax, walk, stretch my muscles and bones.

[](#Tai-Temple)Tai Temple
Following local customs, before going up the mountain, went to Tai Temple to burn incense, bow to Buddha.

[](#South-Gate)South Gate
West wind mixed with clouds and mist along the South Gate ridge, fan-blowing over, dividing Hengshan in two.

Reached the top.

[](#Tired)Tired
