[](#Why-Read-"Clean-Code")Why Read "Clean Code"
Clarifying requirements to the level of detail that machines can execute is what programming does, and this reduction is precisely code. Re-examining some business code I've written, I found it's still a mess. Study "Clean Code" to make my code cleaner. Although insights themselves aren't valuable, learning the relationship between insights and specific decisions for cleaning code in case studies is very helpful to me now.

[](#Seeing-the-Big-in-the-Small)Seeing the Big in the Small
No matter how huge software is written, divinity is in the details.
He who is faithful in little is faithful in much.
Mighty oaks from little acorns grow.
"Faithfulness in little things is not a small thing."
"A place for everything, and everything in its place."
