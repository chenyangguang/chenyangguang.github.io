[](#org-mode-Misc)org-mode Misc
Have been using org-mode for some time. Organize the simplest daily usage.

[](#Self-Service)Self-Service
Because I use [spacemacs](http://spacemacs.org/), if it's **org-mode** related commands, can directly use **Alt+X** to bring up these commands to execute. Or directly **Ctrl+h org** to see the help documentation.

![](/2018-11-25-i-do-not-know/org.png)

[](#GTD)GTD
GTD is Getting Things Done. Goal-oriented completion of plans. Can work with pomodoro timer for better effect.

[](#What-My-org-mode-Is-Doing)What My org-mode Is Doing
My daily plan is to do three worthy things every day, plus a summary. Each thing is divided into three priority levels from high to low: A, B, C, and set start time and deadline time, completion time is recorded when finished. Like this:
